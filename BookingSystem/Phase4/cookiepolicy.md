## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies may be:

- **Session cookies** – deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies** – stored until they expire or are manually deleted
